Education & Experiences

After H.S. graduation, I attended the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis, MN where I studied Accounting and Finance. After my first year of college I enlisted in the Marine Corps. (Reserve) and spent the next year in various parts of California. Upon returning to school I took a job in the Math School IT department. After three more years of school, In May 2015 I received my Bachelor's Degree and celebrated by taking a trip to Norway.

My first job out of college was at Universal Hospital Services as a Financial Analyst. My primary Responsibilities in this role were to cost Hospital Technician labor hours and price service contracts offered to clients. After three years at UHS, I accepted a job at Boeing and moved to Washington. My role at Boeing was a Procurement Financial Analyst and my primary responsibilities were to analyze airplane parts pricing on supplier bids and lead negotiations with prospective suppliers.

After two years at Boeing I decided to change career paths and enrolled in the Masters in Computer Science program at Northeastern University in Seattle. Some classes I have taken include Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Web Development and Data Mining. During my time at Northeastern I have served as a TA for two semesters and have achieved the AWS Cloud Architect Certification.
